Re:Zero kara Hajimeru Isekai Seikatsu

Re:Zero - Starting Life in Another World (Japanese: Ri:Zero kara Hajimeru Isekai Seikatsu) is a Japanese light novel series written by Tappei Nagatsuki and illustrated by Shinichirou Otsuka. The story centers on Subaru Natsuki, a hikikomori who suddenly finds himself transported to another world on his way home from the convenience store.
The series was initially serialized on the website Shousetsuka ni Narou from 2012 onwards.
The 25-episode anime series premiered on April 4, 2016 with an extended 50-minute first episode. The series is directed by Masaharu Watanabe and written by Masahiro Yokotani, with animation by the studio White Fox.
